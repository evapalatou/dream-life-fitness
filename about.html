About Sotiris




Qualified Level 3 Personal Trainer since August 2009

A graduate of the Department of Physical Education and Sports Science (class of 2012) of the University of Thessaly with a 10-year professional experience. In 2018, Sotiris completed his Master's degree at the Department of Physical Education, Sports & Medicine at the University of Thessaly. From a young age, he's intensely involved with sports, while he has been taking part in basketball and swimming competitions with many local and pan-Hellenic distinctions. His career as a fitness trainer started back in 2009, while he attends classes at the School of Physical Education & Sport Science. He has worked for several prestigious fitness centers in Athens and has participated in numerous athletic initiations in central Greece. He strongly recommends physical activity specifically for kids and eldrely. The field of expertise extends to outdoor activities, as well. Interested individuals can easily enjoy extreme sports such as skiing, swimming, cross-training, ping pong, javelin, weightlifting.
